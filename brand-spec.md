## 🎨 辅助资产

### 色板 (Derived from Logo)
- **Primary Red**: #BE0000 (The "Model Mania" red)
- **Accent Orange**: #FF8A00 (Flame orange)
- **Neutral Black**: #1A1A1A (Truck body / Road)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF or very light grey for high contrast

### 字型
- **Display**: 'Outfit', sans-serif (Modern, bold for hobbyist energy)
- **Body**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Clean, readable)

### 气质关键词
- Enthusiastic
- Precise
- Craft-focused
- Nostalgic yet Modern

### 禁区
- No generic "corporate" blues or greens.
- No "slop" gradients that don't serve the flame/hobby theme.
